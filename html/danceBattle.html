DANCE BATTLE

EVENT RULES

  • A team should have 8-18 members.
  • Registration must be done in prior and audio files should be submitted while registering itself.
  • Participants should bring their songs in pendrive.
  • Time duration is 4-8 min.
  • No extra time will be given for the participants.
  • There is no particular theme.
  • Choice of song is open to the participants.
  • Participants should get their own props. All the props must be specified to the coordinator.
  • The teams will be judged on their costumes, visual presentation, and dance form.
  • ID card of the participants is mandatory.
  • The decision of judges will be final.
  • Registration Fees 100/- per head.
